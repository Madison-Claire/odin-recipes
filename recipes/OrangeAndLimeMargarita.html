Orange and Lime Margarita

light, refreshing, not too sweet and destined to be a hit at your next summer BBQ!

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces freshly squeezed orange juice
  • 1 ounce freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1 ounce tequila
  • 1 tablespoon agave syrup
  • ice as needed
  • orange twist

Steps

  1. Pour orange juice, lime juice, tequila and agave syrup into a cocktail shaker. Add ice and shake well. Strain into a margarita glass and serve with an organge twist.
